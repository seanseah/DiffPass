About DiffPass.

Problem: One of the most common security flaw is using the same password for multiple sites. Convenient, I know but yet extremely dangerous. When one site is breached, it immediately put at risk or your other services.

DiffPass lets you create different passwords for differnet sites easily. You just need to remember a single passphrase.

Are my passwords safe?

The passwords are generated inside your web browser itself. None of your data is shared or uploaded and you can even use the tool in offline mode.
